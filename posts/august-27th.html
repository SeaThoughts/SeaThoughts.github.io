My new Apartment in Seattle

Posted on August 27, 2025 by Felipe

First, things first. Let's acknowledge the language that I am writing this in. I am no native speaker of English, yet I find myself in my new apartment in Seattle. It was my decision, it was everything I wanted, and I take every single bit of responsibility for being where I am. Yet, I still have the not so lingering feeling to complain. This is just the reminiscences of my upbringing, and therefore I refuse to fight with it today.

I assume that my family and friends will manage to translate this into Spanish. The ones that don't speak the language I mean. I appreciate you being on my blog. By no means I want you to be entertained, and if you are, it is only a side effect. I might forget this in a couple of days, but today I have the energy and excitement to start something new.

This is that I want to start, is my blog and artistic space on the web. I have a nostalgic love for old websites and I remember starting to create them in high school with my friends at puccinis class. I never understood why he didn't teach us about logic, real programming languages and so on. He only explained html, css and javascript, with javascript being the only real programming language.

Okay, I am sorry I diverted a bit into my thoughts. Let's focus on what I actually wanted to talk about. That one thing is my life in August of 2025. I moved to a new country and my sister visited. The visiting was a surprise for both of us, as we got to know a new version of the people we love. This is not always easy, and many times it comes with its discussions and fights.

Right now, almost everything in my apartment is already laid out. I am only missing a sofa bed for the living room and a shower curtain. The latter didn't prevent me from taking a shower today, which turned out to be a bad idea. My bathroom floor is wood, and I wonder if this is done for saving costs. I almost forgot, I am also missing a toaster. I will try to add photos, so you can see what I am talking about.

Okay. I actually want people to read this, so this might be my last paragraph. Hopefully, next entrance I can focus on an actual topic, and not everything that comes to my mind. I have to develop the art of patience if I actually want to write something I like. Nonetheless, calling this a blog removes that burden of calling this a writing of mine. I hope you understand, what I mean.
