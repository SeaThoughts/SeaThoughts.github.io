The Lost Art of Digital Wandering

Posted on August 25, 2025 by Felipe

Remember when browsing the internet felt like an adventure? When you'd click on a random link and end up three hours deep in someone's personal website about collecting vintage bottle caps?

Those days seem long gone. Now we're trapped in walled gardens, fed content by algorithms that think they know what we want better than we do. The serendipity is gone, replaced by endless scrolling through the same recycled memes and hot takes.

But here's the thing - the old web is still there! Hidden beneath layers of SEO optimization and social media noise, there are still thousands of personal websites, blogs, and digital gardens waiting to be discovered.

I challenge you: next time you're online, resist the urge to open your usual apps. Instead, try:

  • Following a random Wikipedia link chain
  • Browsing through old web directories
  • Checking out someone's personal homepage
  • Reading actual blogs (like this one!)

The internet is still vast and wonderful - we just need to remember how to explore it.
