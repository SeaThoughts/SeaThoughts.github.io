Welcome to UrbanBlog!

Posted on August 27, 2025 by Felipe

Hello internet! This is my first post on my new retro-style blog. I wanted to create something that feels like the early days of the web - simple, personal, and authentic.

You know that feeling when you stumble upon an old GeoCities page or browse through the Wayback Machine? That nostalgic rush of simpler times when the web was just people sharing their thoughts without algorithms or engagement metrics?

That's exactly what I'm trying to recreate here. No tracking, no ads, no comments section drama - just pure, unfiltered thoughts from one human to another.

I'll be sharing my experiences with urban exploration, thoughts on technology, random musings about life, and maybe some terrible poetry. Consider yourself warned! üòÑ
